Creative coding lab Zurich July 5th, 2024

The creative coding lab is a monthly gathering to create beautiful art with code, visualize data, and learn from each other. The event is free and open to all: it's an opportunity to get creative together in a relaxed and friendly environment. Beginners and first-timers should feel especially welcome!

As spaces are limited, please register here for the event (and deregister in case you can't make it anymore): https://ti.to/creative-coding-lab/coding-lab-july

What exactly will we do?

We'll meet for about 3 hours and code together:

18:30 - 19:00 Round of introductions, we present ourselves and what we want to work on. You can work on your project or do a tutorial for some technology you want to learn, see the Resources section for inspiration.
19:00 - 20:30 Coding time. Could be anything from a following a tutorial on how to draw lines in SVG, wrangle some data you saw on the Open Data Zurich site, draw a cool shader animation or flash 100 leds in the rhythm of the music with that arduino board you bought some time ago and never had time to code it.
20:30 - 21:00 Presentations: in case someone wants to quickly share something that they learned or show what they did within the 2 hours.

When and where will we meet?

I recently discovered a super cozy events space in Oerlikon and I booked it for Friday evening, 5th of July starting at 18:30.

Gubelstrasse 50, im Innenhof. Please check the indications on how to get to the Innenhof here.

Who is behind this weird idea?

I'm Raluca and I work as a solutions engineer at Esri, a company where we make software that analyses and visualizes geospatial data. I love coding visual, interactive experiences and I thought it might be fun to get together with other people, leave away our phones and other distractions and just code, learn from each other and discuss possible visualization ideas :)
