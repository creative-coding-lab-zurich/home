Creative coding lab Zurich

The creative coding lab is a monthly gathering to create beautiful art with code, visualize data, and learn from each other. The event is free and open to all, regardless of age, origin, gender or experience. It's an opportunity to get creative together in a relaxed and friendly environment. Beginners and first-timers should feel especially welcome!

What exactly will we do?

We'll meet for about 3 hours and code together. This is a rough schedule:

18:00 - 18:30 Round of introductions, we present ourselves and what we want to work on. We'll also have a challenge for each meetup. You can work on your project or try to solve the challenge. You can also team up with someone and work on it together.
18:30 - 20:30 We work on our project. Could be anything from a following a tutorial on how to draw lines in SVG, wrangle some data you saw on the Open Data Zurich site, draw a cool shader animation, you name it.
20:30 - 21:00 Presentations: in case someone wants to quickly share something that they learned or show what they did within the 2 hours.
