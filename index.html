Creative coding lab Zurich August 25th, 2025

The creative coding lab is a gathering for people who like to create beautiful art with code, visualize data, and learn from each other. The event is open to all: it's an opportunity to get creative together in a relaxed and friendly environment. Beginners and first-timers should feel especially welcome!

What exactly will we do?

We'll meet for about 2 hours, from 17:30 to 19:30 and talk about our (side) projects in data visualization, art and creative coding. You can also just join us to get inspired.

Show us your project or an idea you have, present a technology that got you excited or just tell us a bit about yourself and your interests. We can also use this time to brainstorm on how to solve the issues you run into. We have a large screen you can use to present.

When and where will we meet?

The awesome folks at Interactive Things host us for this gathering on Monday evening, 25th of August starting at 17:30.

Brauerstrasse 37, 8004 Zurich.
